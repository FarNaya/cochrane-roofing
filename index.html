asphalt shingle roof replacement services

A new roof is a significant investment, but one that will pay off over time. Not only is it the first layer of defense against damage to your home, but it can also be an area to show off your style. Your asphalt shingles can come in just about any colour you want. Additionally, they can be designed to complement the siding, soffit, and fascia on your home.

Furthermore, when installed correctly, a new roof will dramatically reduce your heating and cooling costs.

our roof replacement process

Cochrane roofing is a leading roofing contractor, we pride ourselves on being on time and getting a project done within the defined scope. Our process is simple:

Once you decide to reach out, we will come to your home for a free roof assessment.

At this stage, we will discuss what colour of shingles you would like, and any problem points you've had with your roof.
